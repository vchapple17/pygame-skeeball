# Components

220 ohm Resister
1 LED

# Resources

https://gpiozero.readthedocs.io/en/stable/recipes.html

# Breadboard

GROUND (BOARD6) to ground strip

GROUND to short leg LED

LONG Leg LED to 220 Ohm Resister

Other end of 220 Ohm Resister TO BOARD11 (GPIO17)



# OLD

Connect 5V to breadboard power strip

Connect one side of 220ohm resister to 3.3 V lane, then other to an empty lane

Connect +/longer lead of LED to the other end of the 220ohm.

Connect -/short lead of LED to the GPIO0 (pin 11, or pin 17 on GPIO).
